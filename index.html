Origin

Derived from Corgeron layout – Difference: pressed A in Row 3. Focus on modal melodies and Bal Folk.

Strengths by Key

C-Reihe C-Moll und C-Mixolydian
Heim ★ A-Äolisch – durch A in Row 3 gestärkt
